Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

How to get your Akan name.

Step One

  • Type in your date of birth.
  • Type in your Month of birth.
  • Type in your Year of birth.

Step Two

  • Select Your Gender.
  • Look to see if you have filled the spaces correctly.
  • Click the submit button.

Step Three

  • Look at the top of your screen.
  • An alert message will pop-up with your Akan name..
  • Press Ok to exit.
